    Learn more

    • Asset Assessment Mode

      Comprehensive analyze any crypto asset and assess all possible risks associated with that asset. Our advanced Comprehensive Risk Assessment Neural Network provides accurate and comprehensive analysis, allowing you to make informed decisions and safeguard your investments.

    • Portfolio Assessment Mode

      Now you can assess all the risks associated with your entire crypto portfolio using our powerful Comprehensive Risk Assessment Neural Network. Analyze all assets in one place and make informed decisions for more successful management of your portfolio.

    • Comprehensive risk analysis powered by neural network. Our neural network will conduct a detailed analysis of the crypto asset, identifying and assessing all possible risks associated with its use and market conditions. You will get a complete picture of the risks that may affect your asset.

    • Subscribe to risk alerts. You can subscribe to be notified of new risks associated with the selected asset. This will help you to be aware of any changes and react to possible threats in a timely manner.

    • Make informed decisions. With accurate and reliable analysis, you will be able to make informed decisions about your crypto assets, minimizing risks and increasing opportunities for profitable investments.

    • Stake to Vote

      You have the opportunity to stake your ISP tokens in Governance Staking and receive vISP tokens as proof of ownership of staked ISP tokens. With the help of vISP you will be able to participate in proposals for the development and improvement of the project. The more ISP tokens you staked, the more weight your vote carries, and the more influence you will have on decision-making in our community.

      Reputation is an important metric that determines the strength of your voting power and is essentially a multiplier for your vote. Your reputation demonstrates your activity and the integrity of your votes. Reputation ranges from 0.1 to 3, with an initial value of 1. Don't forget to keep track of your reputation and strive to increase it in order to increase the weight of your vote and influence on decision-making.

Your proposal will be available for voting to all members of our community, and if it receives majority support, it will be considered by our team for further implementation.
